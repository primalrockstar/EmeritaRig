# EMT-B App - Installation Guide for Customers

Thank you for purchasing the EMT-B Training App! Follow these simple steps to install and activate your app.

---

## üì± **Android Installation**

### **Step 1: Download the App**
- Check your email for the download link
- Or login to your account at [website]/downloads
- Download the `EMTB-Release.apk` file to your Android device

### **Step 2: Enable Installation**
1. Open **Settings** on your Android device
2. Go to **Security** (or **Privacy**)
3. Enable **Install from Unknown Sources**
   - Or on newer Android: Enable for your browser/file manager

### **Step 3: Install the App**
1. Open your **Downloads** folder
2. Tap on `EMTB-Release.apk`
3. Tap **Install**
4. Wait for installation to complete
5. Tap **Open** or find EMT-B in your app drawer

### **Step 4: Activate Your License**
1. Open the EMT-B app
2. When prompted, enter your license key (from your purchase email)
3. Tap **Activate**
4. Start learning!

## ‚ùì **Troubleshooting**

### **Android Issues**

**"App not installed"**
- Make sure you enabled "Install from Unknown Sources"
- Delete any previous version of the app
- Restart your device and try again

**"Parse error"**
- Download the APK again (file may be corrupted)
- Check that your Android version is compatible (Android 7.0+)

**"Invalid license key"**
- Copy-paste the key (don't type manually)
- Make sure there are no extra spaces
- Check that you're using the correct key from your email

### **iOS Issues**

**"Unable to install"**
- Make sure you have enough storage space
- Check your internet connection
- Try deleting and reinstalling TestFlight

**"App won't open"**
- Check that you trusted the developer certificate
- Settings ‚Üí General ‚Üí VPN & Device Management

**TestFlight invitation expired**
- Contact support for a new invitation
- Invitations last 30 days
