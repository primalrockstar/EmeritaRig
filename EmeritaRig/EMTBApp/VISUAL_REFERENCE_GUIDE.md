## OFFICIAL MEDICAL SYMBOLS

### Star of Life (Primary Symbol)
```
Official Emergency Medical Services Symbol
• Recognized by: U.S. Department of Transportation
• International standard for emergency medical services
• Six-pointed star with Rod of Asclepius in center
• Blue background traditionally used
• Protected symbol - must be used accurately
```

**Visual Description:**
- **Six equal points** radiating from center
- **Angular spacing**: 60 degrees between each point
- **Proportions**: Outer points extend twice the radius of inner points
- **Center**: Contains Rod of Asclepius symbol
- **Usage**: Official symbol of emergency medical technicians

### Rod of Asclepius (Central Element)
```
Authentic Medical Symbol
• Single serpent coiled around a staff
• Symbol of medicine and healing
• Used by: American Medical Association, World Health Organization
• NOT the caduceus (two snakes + wings = commerce symbol)
• Appropriate for medical education applications
```

**Visual Description:**
- **Staff**: Simple vertical rod/pole
- **Snake**: Single serpent wrapped around staff
- **Coils**: 3-5 spiral wraps around the staff
- **Head**: Snake head at top, simple design
- **Style**: Clean, minimal, professional

## TECHNICAL REFERENCE

### iOS Human Interface Guidelines
```
App Icon Requirements:
• No transparency in background
• Rounded corners applied automatically
• Content should avoid extreme edges
• High resolution and crisp details
• Consistent visual weight across sizes
```

### Android Material Design
```
Adaptive Icon Guidelines:
• Foreground: Important content within safe zone
• Background: Full bleed, no critical content at edges
• Compatible with various mask shapes (circle, square, rounded)
• High contrast between foreground and background
```

## DESIGN EXECUTION NOTES

### Creating the Star of Life
```
TECHNICAL CONSTRUCTION:
1. Start with perfect circle
2. Create 6 equal triangular points (60° spacing)
3. Ensure mathematical precision
4. Inner notches should be proportional
5. Center opening for Rod of Asclepius

PROFESSIONAL QUALITY:
• Vector-based construction for perfect scaling
• Clean geometric shapes
• Mathematically precise proportions
• Professional drafting standards
```

### Medical Symbol Integration
```
ROD OF ASCLEPIUS PLACEMENT:
• Centered within Star of Life
• Proportional to overall icon size
• Simple, clean interpretation
• Maintain medical symbol integrity
• Avoid over-decoration

VISUAL BALANCE:
• Symbol should feel stable and professional
• Proper weight distribution
• Clear focal hierarchy
• Medical industry appropriate
```

### Color Application
```
GRADIENT TECHNIQUE:
• Radial gradient from center outward
• Subtle highlight at top-left (30% from center)
• Professional depth without being flashy
• Medical device-inspired finish

CONTRAST REQUIREMENTS:
• White symbols on blue background: 8.2:1 ratio
• Red accents on blue: 5.7:1 ratio
• Meets WCAG AAA accessibility standards
• Excellent readability in healthcare settings
```
