## ICON DESIGN REQUIREMENTS

### Core Symbol: Star of Life
**CRITICAL**: Must use the official "Star of Life" medical symbol
- **Six-pointed star** (not five or eight points)
- **Equal spacing** between all points
- **Clean, geometric design**
- **Professional medical appearance**

**Reference**: The Star of Life is the official symbol of emergency medical services, recognized internationally by healthcare professionals.

### Central Element: Rod of Asclepius
**CRITICAL**: Must be medically accurate
- **Single snake coiled around a staff** (NOT the caduceus with two snakes and wings)
- **Simple, clean interpretation**
- **Appropriate for medical education**
- **Professional healthcare symbolism**

**Note**: The Rod of Asclepius is the authentic symbol of medicine and healing.

### Typography Element
- **"EMS" text** for larger icon sizes
- **Font**: Clean, bold sans-serif (Helvetica Bold or similar)
- **Color**: Pure white (#ffffff)
- **Placement**: Bottom third of icon
- **Effect**: Subtle drop shadow for depth

## DESIGN SPECIFICATIONS

### Layout & Composition

#### Master Icon (1024×1024px)
```
BACKGROUND:
• Circular design with 50px margin from edges
• Radial gradient: #3b82f6 (center) to #1e40af (edges)
• Subtle highlight at top-left (30% from center)
• Professional medical-grade appearance

MAIN SYMBOL:
• Star of Life: 600×600px, centered
• Color: Pure white (#ffffff)
• Rod of Asclepius in center: proportional size
• Clean, professional design

ACCENT ELEMENT:
• Small medical cross (+) in red (#dc2626)
• Size: 80×80px
• Position: Top-right area of star
• White outline for visibility

TYPOGRAPHY:
• "EMS" text at bottom
• Size: 120px font
• Color: White with subtle shadow
• Letter spacing: +2px
```

#### Size Variations
```
LARGE SIZES (152px and above):
• Include all elements (star, rod, text, cross)
• Maintain full detail and effects
• Professional medical appearance

MEDIUM SIZES (76px - 120px):  
• Simplify rod details slightly
• Maintain "EMS" text
• Keep medical cross accent
• Reduce shadow effects

SMALL SIZES (29px - 58px):
• Remove "EMS" text entirely
• Focus on Star of Life symbol only
• Increase contrast for clarity
• Bold, simple shapes only
```

### Android Adaptive Icon Components

#### Foreground Layer (192×192px)
```
• Star of Life symbol ONLY
• Pure white color (#ffffff)
• Centered within safe zone (66dp radius)
• No background elements
• Compatible with various mask shapes
```

#### Background Layer (192×192px)
```
• Medical blue gradient only
• Full bleed design (edge to edge)
• No critical content near edges
• Same gradient as main icon background
```

## MEDICAL SYMBOL ACCURACY

### Star of Life Requirements
- **Exactly 6 points** (medical standard)
- **Equal angular spacing** (60 degrees between points)

- **Simple staff/rod**
- **Medical accuracy essential**
- **Clean, minimal design**

**IMPORTANT**: These symbols have specific medical meanings. Accuracy is critical for healthcare professional credibility.

## DO'S AND DON'TS

### ✅ DO:
- Use the exact Star of Life symbol (6 points)
- Use Rod of Asclepius (single snake, no wings)
- Maintain medical accuracy and professionalism
- Ensure high contrast for accessibility
- Test at smallest sizes for clarity
- Follow provided color specifications exactly
- Create clean, professional medical appearance

### ❌ DON'T:
- Use caduceus symbol (two snakes + wings)
- Use 5-pointed star or other star variations
- Add unnecessary decorative elements
- Use non-medical colors or themes
- Make it look like a game or consumer app
- Use copyrighted medical symbols
- Compromise medical symbol accuracy
